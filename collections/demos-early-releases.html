Various unofficial Bassmint Productions demo tapes circulate, drawing from two CD-Rs sold by DJ Butterfingers on eBay, along with other leaked material. The Slim Shady LP demo CD, titled Untitled Sequence, is a fan-made compilation created after a Slim Shady LP session tape (Ruff Mixes #2) leaked and is considered unofficial.
