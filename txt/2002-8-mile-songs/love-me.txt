Trivia
“Love Me” marks the first song to feature both Eminem and 50 Cent.
The female vocals on the chorus were not recorded for the song. They were taken from VariPhrase Sound Library produced by Roland.
Although the album credits Eminem first, the single was released under Obie Trice’s catalogue, with his name listed first.
