Trivia
The name alludes to the novel Rabbit, Run by John Updike. The screenplay for 8 Mile also opens with a quote from the novel, spoken by the title character Harry “Rabbit” Angstrom: “If you have the guts to be yourself, other people’ll pay your price.”
