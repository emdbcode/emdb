Trivia
“Detroit vs. Everybody” was released as the album’s fourth single—or third if excluding “’Till It’s Gone,” which was included as a bonus track on some versions of the album.
The song’s title is based on the lifestyle brand founded in 2012 by Detroit native and graphic designer Tommey Walker.
