Trivia
“Guts Over Fear” was released as the album’s first single on August 24, 2014, peaking at No. 22 on the Billboard Hot 100 and No. 10 on the UK Singles Chart.

The music video was released three months after the single, on November 24, the same day as the album’s release.
The song marks the second Eminem song to feature Sia, following “Beautiful Pain,” included on the deluxe version of <em>The Marshall Mathers LP 2</em>.

The song can be heard in the trailer and during the end credits of the film <em>The Equalizer</em>, starring Denzel Washington.
