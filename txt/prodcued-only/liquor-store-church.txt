Trivia
Horseshoe Gang is a hip-hop group consisting of the younger brothers of West Coast legend KXNG Crooked — Dice, Demetrius, Julius, and Kenny. The group later shortened their name to Shoe Gang.
The song was released alongside the Eminem-produced “Hot Sauce” by KXNG Crooked featuring Horseshoe Gang, credited as Family Bvsiness — the collective name for Horseshoe Gang and KXNG Crooked.
“Liquor Store Church Freestyle,” an alternate version of this song, featuring different lyrics and no appearances from KXNG Crooked and Dice, was released on August 27, 2021, on the group’s Eminem-dedicated album <em>Shady Past</em>.
