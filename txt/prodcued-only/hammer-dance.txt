Trivia
The song was released as the album’s first single, premiering on Funkmaster Flex on February 27, 2012, before being released to iTunes on March 13, 2012.
The song was later included on the Greatest Hits disc of the Shady Records compilation album <em>SHADYXV</em>.
The song’s title is a reference to a 1990s hip-hop dance move popularized by MC Hammer, primarily in his “U Can’t Touch This” music video.
