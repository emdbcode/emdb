Trivia
2Pac’s and A.B. Money’s vocals were recorded circa 1993. The song was intended to appear on the Thug Life album in 1994 but was omitted, along with several other tracks, due to censorship issues.
2Pac performed the song at the The Source Awards. A production error occurred when the song began playing before A Tribe Called Quest had finished their acceptance speech, cutting them off. The incident led to a brief dispute between 2Pac and the group, who initially believed the interruption was deliberate and disrespectful.
