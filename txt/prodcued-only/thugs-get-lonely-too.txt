Trivia
“Thugs Get Lonely Too” was released as the album’s first single, peaking at No. 98 on the Billboard Hot 100.
2Pac’s vocals were recorded in 2003, while Nate Dogg’s were recorded in 2004.
The original version features a prominent sample of “If I Was Your Girlfriend” by Prince, along with additional chorus vocals by Mopreme Shakur and Macadoshis of Thug Life.
In 1997, after 2Pac’s death, Quincy Jones III suggested to Tech N9ne that he record a verse for the song, which he did. This version was never officially released, but it later leaked and features the original Live Squad beat.
