Trivia
“On Fire” was released as Lloyd Banks’s debut single. The song peaked at No. 8 on the Billboard Hot 100 and No. 18 on the UK Singles Chart.
Words from 50 Cent
I would be able to look at someone else’s record and make my version of it when the right production came, and it would be a hit again — like when Nelly’s going, “It’s getting hot in here, so take off all your clothes…” That’s how I gave Banks “On Fire.” It’s the same concept. It’s the same record — just new melodies, new production. — 50 Cent, Hot 97 FM, 2024
