Trivia
2Pac’s vocals were recorded circa 1992. Mopreme Shakur and Mouse Man were originally featured but were omitted from Eminem’s version.
“Never Ignorant Getting Goals Accomplished” is a backronym for the word N.I.G.G.A., associated with 2Pac. However, the album’s tracklist incorrectly lists the title as “Never Ignorant About Getting Goals Accomplished,” inserting “About” in error. The intended phrasing is meant to be split into two parts: “Never Ignorant” / “Getting Goals Accomplished.”
