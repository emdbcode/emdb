Trivia
“Rap Name” is the debut single by Obie Trice. The song peaked at No. 60 on the Billboard Hot R&B/Hip-Hop Singles Sales chart.
The song was sampled in Eminem’s “Without Me” months before its official release. A “Without Me” sample was then added to “Rap Name” for its release.
The song is based on the fact that Obie Trice uses his legal name as his rap name.
The song’s video features cameos from Eminem and D12, excluding Bizarre.
