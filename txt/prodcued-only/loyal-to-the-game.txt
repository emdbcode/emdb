Trivia
• The song’s original version was officially released in 1994, first on the cassette edition of the <em>Above The Rim</em> soundtrack, and a month later on Warren G and Nate Dogg’s “Regulate” single. It has since been included on digital editions of the <em>Above the Rim</em> soundtrack.
<p>The original version featured Treach of Naughty by Nature and The Riddler, who were omitted from Eminem’s version.
• DJ Quik produced a remix of the song, which appears as a bonus track on the album.
