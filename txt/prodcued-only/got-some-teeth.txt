Trivia
“Got Some Teeth” was released as the album’s lead single, charting at No. 54 on the Billboard Hot 100 and No. 8 on the UK Singles Chart.
The song’s video features cameos from Eminem and D12, excluding Bizarre.
In the video, Eminem — playing a bartender — serves Obie a bottle of beer called “Trife Life,” a reference to the Mobb Deep song.
