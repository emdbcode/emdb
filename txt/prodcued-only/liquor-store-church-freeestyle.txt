Trivia
Shoe Gang, formerly Horseshoe Gang, is a hip-hop group consisting of the younger brothers of West Coast legend KXNG Crooked — Dice, Demetrius, Julius, and Kenny.
This is an alternate version of the group’s 2020 single “Liquor Store Church”, featuring different lyrics and no appearances from KXNG Crooked and Dice.
