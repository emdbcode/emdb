Trivia
“You Don’t Know” was released as the album’s lead single.
Although the song is cataloged under Eminem’s discography, 50 Cent is credited first on the album.
In song’s video, Eminem appears in a straitjacket and mask, being transported in a manner reminiscent of Hannibal Lecter from The Silence of the Lambs. The film was later heavily referenced on Eminem’s next album, Relapse.
An edited, one-verse version of the video, featuring Detroit Tigers highlights, was broadcast to open Game 2 of the 2006 World Series, when the Tigers defeated the St. Louis Cardinals 3–1 at Comerica Park.
The song can be heard in the WWE movie The Condemned, starring “Stone Cold” Steve Austin as a condemned killer who must fight to the death against nine others on a desolate island. It was also used in the film’s trailer but was not included on the soundtrack album.
