Trivia
A Swift-heavy track, Swifty performs the chorus and two verses, while Kuniva, Kon Artis, and Proof each perform one verse.
Swift can first be heard calling himself an instigator on The Marshall Mathers LP track “Under the Influence.”
On this song, a beef is jokingly portrayed between Kon Artis and Proof.
