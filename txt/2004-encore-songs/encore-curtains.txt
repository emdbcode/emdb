Trivia
“Encore” was only released in the United States as a promotional single. In the United Kingdom, however, these promos were sold as standard physical singles, allowing the song to chart on the UK Hip-Hop and R&B Singles Chart. It is therefore counted as the album’s third single, following “Just Lose It” and “Mosh.”
The album version includes an attached skit, “Curtains,” performed by Eminem. On digital platforms, “Curtains” has since been retitled “Curtains Down.”
In the attached skit, Shady shoots himself in an apparent suicide attempt, then says “See you in hell” using the electrolarynx heard earlier in the skit “Em Calls Paul.” However, the exact events remain open to interpretation. One theory suggests that Shady is actually shot by Christopher Reeve, who then delivers the album’s closing line—this idea stems from Reeve being heavily mocked on the album, even after the omitted track “Christopher Reeves,” giving him a motive for revenge. Reeve later appears in “Medicine Ball” on Eminem’s next studio album, Relapse, where he declares, “Eminem, I'm coming to kill you.”
