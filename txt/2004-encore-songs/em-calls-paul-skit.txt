Trivia
This skit marks the first time a “Paul” phone call receives a reply.
The device Eminem uses to change his voice is an electrolarynx, a handheld device held against the throat. The electrolarynx is notably used by the animated character Ned Gerblansky from South Park, a show Eminem has referenced on many occasions.
A second “Em Calls Paul” skit appears on his tenth studio album, Kamikaze.
