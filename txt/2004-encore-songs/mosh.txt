Trivia

“Mosh” was released as the album’s second single, following “Just Lose It,” and failed to chart due to limited radio play and the lack of a physical single release, despite physical singles still being popular outside the US at the time.

On October 26, one week after the song’s release and one week before the 2004 presidential election, the video, produced by Guerrilla News Network, debuted on the company’s website. Created to encourage voters to vote George W. Bush out of office, it aired on MTV the following day and immediately topped the channel’s “Hot Video” chart. The video’s style is similar to that of “White America,” which was also produced by Guerrilla News Network.
