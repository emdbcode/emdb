Trivia
“Mockingbird” was released as the album’s fifth single, surprising some fans who expected “Ass Like That” to be next, as it had been featured on the album’s hype sticker while “Mockingbird” had not. “Ass Like That” was released as the following single.
“Mockingbird” won Best Rap Track at the 2005 Teen Choice Awards.
The song has become one of Eminem’s most‑streamed songs, surpassing over 2 billion streams on Spotify and ranking among the top five most popular tracks in his catalogue.
