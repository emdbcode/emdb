Trivia
“Puke” is a song aimed at Kim Scott, Eminem’s ex-wife, but in early live performances Eminem often twisted the intro to be about Mariah Carey while still performing the original song. In one performance, he began with: “Mariah Carey, we had some good times together, didn’t we? Remember the time we were dry-humping, and you were calling me Superman, and then I took the mask off your face, and I rolled over, and I was like, ‘Oh my God, I’ma fuckin’ be sick.’”
In a 2005 live performance of the song, it was reported that the Carey voicemails referenced in Eminem’s song “The Warning” were played as an exclusive live intro to “Puke,” featuring the singer pleading: “I heard you were getting back with your ex-wife. Why won't you see me? Why won't you call me?”
The song samples the drum beat from “We Will Rock You” by Queen. Eminem previously used the sample on his previous album, The Eminem Show, on the track “‘Till I Collapse.”
