Trivia
This freestyle was issued as a non-album promotional release for his forthcoming album <em>Recovery</em>. It premiered on Shade 45 and was then posted on Rap Radar the same day.
The song was released the same day as “Not Afraid,” the lead single from <em>Recovery</em>.
