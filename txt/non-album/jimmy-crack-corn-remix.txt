Trivia
This vocal remix, released as a non-album single, features a four-bar intro by Eminem absent from the original version. 
Although not included on the album, its digital release uses the <em>Re-Up</em> cover art; the original version, featuring 50 Cent instead of Ca$his, appears on the album.
