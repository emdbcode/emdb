Trivia
“Hit ’Em Up” is a diss track aimed at former House of Pain frontman Everlast, a.k.a. Whitey Ford, in retaliation to Everlast’s track “Whitey’s Revenge,” which itself was a response to Eminem’s “I Remember,” released in response to subliminal disses from Everlast on the Dilated Peoples track “Ear Drums Pop (Remix).”
The song originally leaked in 2000 as part of “Quitter,” another diss aimed at Everlast, with both recorded back-to-back in the same studio session. Neither was officially recognised until the release of the <em>Shady Classics Mixtape</em> in 2014, when they were each featured separately. A bootleg single released in 2000 is commonly mistaken for an official release.
