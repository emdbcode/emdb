Trivia
“My Fault (Pizza Mix)” is a revised, drug-free version of the original track found on <em>The Slim Shady LP</em>.
This version was recorded in March 1999, after the release of <em>The Slim Shady LP</em>, and was officially announced as a potential follow-up single to “My Name Is.” By late April, those plans were scrapped, and the song instead replaced “Bad Influence” on the MTV Celebrity Deathmatch soundtrack on November 2, 1999. In honor of its inclusion, a claymation music video was reportedly in development, but it ultimately went unreleased.
Unlike the original version, Eminem is not credited as a co-producer.
