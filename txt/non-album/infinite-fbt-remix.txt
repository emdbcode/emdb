This remix was released as a non-album single to celebrate the 20th anniversary of Eminem’s debut album, <em>Infinite</em>. The song peaked at No. 97 on the Billboard Hot 100.

The original version was produced by D12’s Denaun Porter. This version is produced by Jeff and Mark Bass, who co-produced many of Eminem’s songs from <em>The Slim Shady LP</em> and <em>The Marshall Mathers LP</em>, with Jeff continuing up until <em>Relapse</em> in 2009.

The Bass Brothers’ approach, as they put it, was: “What would it be like to hear the <em>Infinite</em> album with no samples, and take the approach that we used for all of the [later] Eminem music?” To find out, they applied the same treatment used on subsequent Eminem tracks by replaying the instrumental with live instruments.

Although the Bass Brothers remixed more of the <em>Infinite</em> album than just this title track, none of the others have been released.
