Trivia
The track was officially credited without the “Remix” subtitle, despite being a reworked version of the original track from the <em>Slim Shady EP</em>.

The remix was initially produced for inclusion on <em>The Slim Shady LP</em>, but was ultimately omitted.

Earlier remixes from the <em>Slim Shady LP</em> sessions have since leaked — one resembling the original EP version, and another resembling this “Next Friday” remix, featuring the Swift vocals and 2Pac sample from the EP version.
