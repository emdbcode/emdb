Trivia
“Doe Rae Me” is essentially a D12 song featuring Obie Trice dissing Ja Rule in retaliation for him dissing Eminem’s daughter on the song “Loose Change.” Eminem does not perform a verse but provides the hook, intro, and outro.
The song is often mistaken for an <em>Encore</em> outtake due to its inclusion on the unofficial mixtape <em>Straight From The Lab</em>, which features leaked <em>Encore</em> tracks that were initially planned for the then-forthcoming album. However, “Doe Rae Me” had already been officially released at that point. The only difference between the official version and the leaked version, dubbed “Hailie’s Revenge,” is that the leaked version includes the full original intro, which was cut short on the official release and replaced with a snippet of Ja Rule.
