This track was first released exclusively on its own CD within <em>The Singles</em> box set, posed as a promotional single. It was later included on the <em>Shady Classics Mixtape</em> in 2014.
The original 50 Cent version was first released as a mixtape track, but Eminem thought it was too strong to be a throwaway, so he used it for the <em>8 Mile</em> soundtrack with a new mix by Dr. Dre and insisted it be included as a bonus track on 50 Cent’s debut album, <em>Get Rich Or Die Tryin’</em>.
Eminem does not perform over the official “Wanksta” instrumental, but over an open loop taken from 50 Cent’s original mixtape version.
