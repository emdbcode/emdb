Trivia
On this freestyle diss track, Eminem addresses the “racist tape,” an early recording of him using the N-word, released by then-CEO of <em>The Source</em> magazine, Benzino, in an attempt to discredit him during the height of their feud. He dove deeper into the subject on the song “Yellow Brick Road” from <em>Encore</em>, released later the same year.
