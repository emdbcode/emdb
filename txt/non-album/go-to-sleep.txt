Trivia
In “Go to Sleep,” Eminem disses Benzino following the release of his diss track “Die Another Day” in their continued back-and-forth. He also warns Ja Rule in response to comments he made in interviews about Eminem and Dr. Dre allowing 50 Cent to release diss tracks against him.
