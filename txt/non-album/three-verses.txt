Trivia
“Three Verses” was released for free exclusively through eminem.com shortly after the release of <em>The Slim Shady LP</em>.
The track is made up of three studio recorded freestyles that were recorded after signing with Dr. Dre and Interscope Records.
The three verses were originally released separately in 1998 on <em>Stretch Armstrong Presents: Eminem’s The Slim Shady LP Snippet Tape</em>, titled “Verse 1, 2 and 3.”
Each beat heard in the freestyles were originally produced by Dr. Dre.
The first and third verses were later released separately in acapella form on <em>The Slim Shady LP (Special Edition)</em> on November 15, 1999, as “Hazardous Youth” and “Greg,” respectively. They were also included on the album’s <em>Expanded Edition</em> in 2019.
Eminem had previously performed the second and third freestyles on a jam-packed episode of <em>The Wake Up Show</em> in 1997, before signing with Dr. Dre and Interscope Records.
