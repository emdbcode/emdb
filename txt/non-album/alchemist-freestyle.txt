Trivia
This freestyle was one of three performed back-to-back on Tim Westwood’s BBC Radio 1 show on May 23, 2009. The footage was made available to stream on YouTube on May 27, 2009, via Tim Westwood’s official channel.
The “happy birthday” intro was added in 2014 for the mixtape’s release.
