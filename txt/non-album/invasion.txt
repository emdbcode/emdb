Trivia
“Invasion” is interchangeably aimed at both Ja Rule and Benzino, who were feuding with Eminem over separate issues.
The song marks the first entry in the Eminem / Ja Rule beef and was released the month after Ja Rule’s interview in <em>The Source</em>, in which he stated: “If you're gonna be authorizing [50 Cent] to spew records on me, then I wanna do what I gotta do to take your company under. And that goes for Dre, Em, or whoever.”
The song came roughly a month after Benzino unexpectedly dropped two Eminem diss tracks in just over a week. 
The song is one of three Eminem tracks aimed at Benzino on the mixtape, along with “The Sauce” and “Nail In The Coffin,” which are more directly aimed at him.
