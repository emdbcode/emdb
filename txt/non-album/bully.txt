Trivia

“Bully” was initially set to appear on Eminem’s fifth studio album <em>Encore</em> but was omitted after the song leaked in November 2003. DJ Whoo Kid was then given the go-ahead to feature it on his <em>G-Unit Radio</em> mixtape, which was released in conjunction with the EU release of the video game <em>SWAT: Global Strike Team</em> a couple of weeks later.

The song is a diss track aimed at former The Source CEO Benzino.
