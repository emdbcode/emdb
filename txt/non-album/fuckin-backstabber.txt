Trivia
“Fuckin Backstabber” is a diss track aimed at fellow Detroit rapper and former collaborator Champtown. The song was written amid rumors that Champtown had attempted to make a romantic advance toward Eminem’s then-girlfriend, Kim Scott.

At this point, Eminem was still spelling his name as M&M.

The release marks both the first official collaboration between Eminem and Proof and Eminem's last collaboration as part of Soul Intent, along with its B-side, "Biterphobia"
