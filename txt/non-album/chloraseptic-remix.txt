Trivia
“Chloraseptic (Remix)” was released as a non-album single on January 8, 2018, and failed to chart on Billboard’s major charts.

The song is Eminem’s first retaliation to the backlash of his then-latest album, <em>Revival</em>, and, in terms of both content and rapping style, is more in the vein of his next album, <em>Kamikaze</em>, even though it’s a remix of a <em>Revival</em> track.

Words from Eminem
It was interesting to watch the reaction to the “Chloraseptic” remix verse that I did […] I watched people going, “Oh, why wasn’t this on the album?” Honestly? What if I said it was on Revival? You won’t know because you didn’t listen, probably. But I kind of felt like, “Okay, I guess this is what people might want,” ’cause I’m always stuck in that “What the fuck do people want?” — Eminem, The Kamikaze Interview, 2018
