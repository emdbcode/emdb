Trivia
“Any Man” was released as a single from the Rawkus Records compilation album <em>Soundbombing II</em>.
The song is mentioned by the title character in Eminem’s song “Stan.”
The album version of the song is cut short, with the single version being the full version. However, the album includes “Any Man (Intro)” — a separate instrumental featuring Eminem’s voice scratched over the beat.
The original version, titled “Fucking Crazy,” appeared on the Slim Shady Demo ’97 in 1997, recorded shortly after the <em>Slim Shady EP</em>.
