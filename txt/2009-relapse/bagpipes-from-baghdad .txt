Trivia

The first two verses of this song are aimed at Mariah Carey and her then-husband Nick Cannon. The third verse, however, is about having sex with vegetated, lesbian conjoined twins.

The song led Mariah Carey to release the diss track “Obsessed,” which in turn led Eminem to respond with “The Warning.”
