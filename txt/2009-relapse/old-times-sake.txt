Trivia

“Old Time’s Sake” was released as the album’s fourth single, following “Crack A Bottle,” “We Made You,” and “3 a.m.”

The song marks the sixth Eminem track to feature Dr. Dre, following “Crack A Bottle,” which appears later on the album but was released as a single prior to it.
