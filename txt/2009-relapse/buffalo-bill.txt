Trivia
Buffalo Bill is a fictional character and the main antagonist of Thomas Harris’s 1988 novel The Silence of the Lambs and its 1991 film adaptation, in which he is played by Ted Levine. The bridge in “3 a.m.,” where Eminem says, “She puts the lotion in the bucket / It puts the lotion on the skin / Or else it gets the hose again,” interpolates dialogue spoken by Buffalo Bill in the film.
