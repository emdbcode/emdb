Trivia

“Hell Breaks Loose” was released as a single on the same day as “Elevator” to promote the album’s re-release.
In a 2022 interview with Paul Rosenberg, Eminem confirmed that the songs released on Relapse: Refill were scheduled to appear on his unreleased album Relapse 2.
