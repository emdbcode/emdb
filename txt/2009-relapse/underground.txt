Words from Paul Rosenberg

“Underground,” that was one of my favourites because [Em was] just spitting like [he was] back at the hip-hop shop. It was sick, and I was so happy that [he] wasn’t brain-damaged [from the overdose] and disabled from rapping like that again. When I listen to it now, it still gives me chills. — Paul Rosenberg, Paul Pod: Curtain Call 2, 2022

Trivia

“Underground” marks the return of Ken Kaniff after not appearing on Eminem’s previous album, Encore. The character appears in a skit attached to the end of the song, closing the album.
