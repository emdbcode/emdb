Trivia

The song’s first verse follows on from abducting a woman named Tonya in the album’s previous track. The second and third verses tell the story of Slim’s earlier victims, a movie star named Lindsey and a pop star named Britney—clear references to Lindsay Lohan and Britney Spears.

At the time of the song’s release, some fans and critics felt the references to Britney Spears and Lindsay Lohan were a little outdated. In a conversation with The New York Times, when asked about it, Dr. Dre said: “Once he makes a painting, once he lays a lyric down, it’s impossible to get him to change it. If there’s a couple of lines he says on a record that’s not relevant today, it’s, ‘No, that was that painting. That was for that moment.’”
