Trivia

“My Mom” is an exaggerated story about drug use within Eminem’s household growing up. The song arrived ten years after “My Name Is,” in which Eminem said he told his mother he would “grow up to be a famous rapper / Make a record about doing drugs and name it after her.”

In 2024, the song was released as the B-side to 3 a.m. on the song’s limited edition physical single.
