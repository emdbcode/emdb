Trivia

“Crack A Bottle” was released as the album’s first single.

On February 12, 2009, the song broke the first-week digital sales record with 418,000 downloads, surpassing the previous record set by T.I.’s “Live Your Life.”

Although the song’s video went into production in February 2009, it wasn’t officially released until August 2022. The video was used to promote Eminem’s second greatest hits album, Curtain Call 2.

A snippet of the video was first seen on a TV in the “3 a.m.” video—both videos directed by Syndrome.
