Trivia
This crank call marks the first skit we hear Eminem perform as Ken Kaniff. The character appeared a few months later on <em>The Slim Shady LP</em>, voiced by Aristotle. Every subsequent appearance of Ken Kaniff in Eminem’s discography has been voiced by Eminem himself.
