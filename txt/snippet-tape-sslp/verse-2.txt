Trivia
• “Verse 2” is a studio rendition of Eminem’s “Greg” freestyle, recorded after Eminem signed to Interscope Records. The acapella was later released on <em>The Slim Shady LP (Special Edition)</em> in November 1999 under its original freestyle title.

• Eminem had previously performed this freestyle live in 1997 on a jam-packed episode of <em>The Wake Up Show</em>, rapping over “Kick In The Door” by The Notorious B.I.G.

• Shortly after the release of <em>The Slim Shady LP</em>, “Verse 2” was bundled with “Verse 1” and “Verse 3” and released as a free MP3 on eminem.com under the title “Three Verses.”
