Trivia

• “Verse 3” is a studio rendition of Eminem’s “Hazardous Youth” freestyle, recorded after Eminem signed to Interscope Records. The acapella was later released on <em>The Slim Shady LP (Special Edition)</em> in November 1999 under its original freestyle title.

• Eminem also performed this freestyle live on <em>The Wake Up Show</em> alongside Akinyele. He also performed the “Verse 1” freestyle during the same session.

• Shortly after the release of <em>The Slim Shady LP</em>, “Verse 3” was bundled with “Verse 1” and “Verse 2” and released as a free MP3 on eminem.com under the title “Three Verses.”
