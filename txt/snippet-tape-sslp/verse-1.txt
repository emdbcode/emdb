Trivia
• “Verse 1” is a studio rendition of an Eminem freestyle, recorded after he signed to Interscope Records. The acapella was later released on <em>The Slim Shady LP (Special Edition)</em> in November 1999 under its original freestyle title.

• Shortly after the release of <em>The Slim Shady LP</em>, “Verse 1” was bundled with “Verse 2” and “Verse 3” and released as a free MP3 on eminem.com under the title “Three Verses.”

• Eminem also performed this freestyle live on <em>The Wake Up Show</em> alongside Akinyele. He also performed the “Verse 2” freestyle during the same session, which was later recorded under its original freestyle title, “Hazardous Youth.”

• Unlike its companion freestyles, “Verse 2” and “Verse 3,” this freestyle’s acapella was not included on The Slim Shady LP (Special Edition) or any subsequent releases.
