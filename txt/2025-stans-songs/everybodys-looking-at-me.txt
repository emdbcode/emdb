Trivia
“Everybody's Looking At Me” was Recorded circa 2001 and remained unreleased until 2025.
A snippet of the song was first heard on <em>The Eminem Show</em> in the skit “The Kiss.”
Eminem borrowed parts of the first verse for his 2002 freestyle on Hot 97 with Funkmaster Flex, featuring Proof, to promote his fourth studio album <em>The Eminem Show</em>.
