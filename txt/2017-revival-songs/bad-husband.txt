Trivia
“Bad Husband” is the second Eminem song to feature X Ambassadors, following “Wicked Ways,” which was included on the deluxe edition of <em>The Marshall Mathers LP 2</em>.
The song is Eminem’s apology to his ex-wife, Kim Scott, whom he had previously scorned on songs like “’97 Bonnie and Clyde,” “Kim,” and “Puke.”
