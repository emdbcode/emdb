Trivia
Released a couple of months after the BET Cypher, where Eminem attacked Donald Trump, “Like Home” is another anti-Trump diss, framed as a patriotic anthem.

The people that support [Donald Trump] are the people he cares about the least, and they don't even realize it. At what point do you — a working-class citizen, someone who's trying to make shit better for you and your family — think this guy, who’s never known struggle his entire fucking life, who avoided the military because of bone spurs, who says he's a billionaire, is really looking out for you? He's got people brainwashed. — Eminem, Vulture, 2017
