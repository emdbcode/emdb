Trivia
“Nowhere Fast” was released as the album’s third single, or fourth if including “Untouchable,” which was released as a promotional single, and failed to chart on any of Billboard’s major charts.
Although released as a single, the song did not receive a music video.
The single version features an intro verse absent from the album version.
Words from Eminem

There’s a lot of shit I say in jest / That is tough to swallow.” You know, there’s a book called Truly Tasteless Jokes; it’s all fucked up shit; it makes me laugh — and that kind of stuff is where my brain goes. I’m not saying I’ve never gone too far, but people shouldn’t be looking to me for political correctness. — Eminem, Vulture, 2017
