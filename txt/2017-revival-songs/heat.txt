Trivia
The song samples actor Mark Wahlberg from the film <em>Boogie Nights</em> in the chorus. Eminem had previously dissed Wahlberg on <em>The Marshall Mathers LP</em> track “Drug Ballad,” calling him a faggot. However, Wahlberg promoted “Heat” days after its release on GQ’s <em>Replies To Fans On The Internet</em>, calling it “a very good song” and admitting he used to be an Eminem hater because Eminem was a better rapper than him.
