Trivia
“Walk on Water” was released as the album’s lead single on November 10, 2017.
Although the song received negative reviews from critics and the general public, it performed fairly well, peaking at No. 14 on the Billboard Hot 100 and No. 7 on the UK Singles Chart.
In December 2017, Denaun Porter revealed that Adele was the first choice to sing the song’s chorus, but due to throat problems, she couldn’t do it.
The original demo version, with Skylar Grey singing the chorus rather than Beyoncé, was released in January 2026 on the vinyl version of <em>STANS (The Official Soundtrack)</em>.
Words from Eminem
Skylar sent [a demo version] to Rick, and Rick was playing it but not for me — he was just playing it — and I walked in. I was like, “What the fuck is that?” […] When he played it for me, I instantly started thinking of lines to it. I was like, “Yo, give me that and I’ll record it in the other room.” So I sat there and wrote it and recorded it pretty quick. […] Once I laid it, we kinda knew we shouldn’t put drums in it. […] I think it was [Rick’s] idea to try to get Beyoncé to do it, and I was like, “Man, she ain’t doing that shit — there’s no fucking way.” She’s really big, she’s got a lot going on, so I just kind of thought it was a stab in the dark — it was like a fantasy thing if we could get her. […] Yeah man, she killed it. — Eminem, Paul Pod: Curtain Call 2, 2022
That song was more about just self-doubt, insecurities, that kind of shit. I’ve never really seen myself as, how can I say…The way I’ve seen some fans act towards me. Even to this day, I’ve been in this game a long time and it’s still weird to me, because I just rap and I certainly haven’t had a perfect career. I’ve put out some not-so-great albums and I realize that. — Eminem, Complex, 2017
