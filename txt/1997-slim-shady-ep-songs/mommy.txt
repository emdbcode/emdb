Words From Jeff Bass
All the sound effects – the crickets, the street noise – we threw a microphone out on 8 Mile Road and started recording it. We were doing the trunk sound, everything. You can [purchase] sound effects like that, but we went out there at 3–4 o’clock in the morning and just put a microphone out there.
— Jeff Bass, Connection Is Magic with Samson Shulman, 2017
