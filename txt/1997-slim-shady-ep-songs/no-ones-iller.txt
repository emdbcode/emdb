Words from Eminem
Me, Swift, Bizzare, Fuzz, and Proof were the illest emcees in Detroit. One time we found ourselves with access to a studio. So we decided to cut a song together. I was trying to get a hold of Proof and couldn't find him. I called Bizzare and he said, "Yo, get Swift on it," so we did. And then we wanted to get Fuzz on the track, 'cause Fuzz was just ridiculous. Proof wasn't available for the song, and besides, he didn't get along with Fuzz. […] Bizzare thought of the hook when he heard the beat. […] It all went in a perfect order. Each verse was iller than the next. It was such a buildup song. That's the vibe we plan on capturing on D-12's debut album. — Eminem, Angry Blonde, 2000
