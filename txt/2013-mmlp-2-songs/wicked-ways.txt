Trivia
“Wicked Ways” marks the return of Ken Kaniff after not appearing on Eminem’s previous album, <em>Recovery</>. The character appears in a skit attached to the end of the song, closing the album. The skit is attached to “Evil Twin” on the standard digital version of the album.
X Ambassadors are credited only on digital versions of the song.
