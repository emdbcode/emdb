Trivia
“Beautiful Pain” features Sia shortly before her worldwide breakthrough with “Chandelier,” which was released in March 2014.
Sia faced some criticism for collaborating with Eminem, whose lyrics—often delivered through his alter ego Slim Shady—have included language that could be interpreted as homophobic. In response, Sia defended Eminem in an interview with <em>The Huffington Post</em>, saying she viewed Slim Shady as a form of performance art and did not believe Eminem himself was homophobic.
