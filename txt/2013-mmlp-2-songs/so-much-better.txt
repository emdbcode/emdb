Trivia
“So Much Better” features the first beat produced by Eminem and Luis Resto since “Elevator” from <em>Relapse: Refill</em>, and only the second since “Pistol Poppin’” by Ca$his in 2007.
The song’s beat is reminiscent of “Criminal” from <em>The Marshall Mathers LP</em>.
