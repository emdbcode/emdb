Trivia
The song was mainly promoted through the release of its video on Mother’s Day, May 11, 2014. It was serviced to radio sometime that month but did not receive an official physical or downloadable single release.
The song is a heartfelt apology to Eminem’s mother, Debbie Mathers, whom Eminem had harshly criticized in earlier tracks, like "My Name Is," "Kill You," and "Cleaning Out My Closet.”
The music video depicts Eminem reuniting with his mother, with an actress portraying Debbie Mathers.
