Trivia
“Bad Guy” serves as sequel to “Stan” from The Marshall Mathers LP.
In 2025, to celebrate 25 years since the release of “Stan,” the song was reissued on 7-inch vinyl, with “Bad Guy” serving as the B-side.
Words from Eminem
I know that “Legacy” was the first song I recorded [for the album], and I think I had about five or six songs, and [Paul] had said something to the effect of, “This sounds like the Mathers LP.” So I thought, “Oh, well that could be crazy,” but I always feel like I need at least one holy shit moment. I just kept racking my brain, like, “What the fuck can I talk about?” And then I had to go back, listen to [The Marshall Mathers LP], and then I thought, “You know, it’d be crazy if Stan is dead but his little brother came back.” And I was trying to make it so that it almost sounded like a relationship song. […] The goal was to make you think I'm talking to a chick to try to get revenge for fucking me over. Then I need one line for you to be like, “Oh, wait — what the fuck!” So the song’s taking you on this journey, but then you realise, “Oh shit, he’s not — this ain’t him — it’s Matthew talking to him.” That was the holy shit moment. — Eminem, Paul Pod: Curtain Call 2, 2022
