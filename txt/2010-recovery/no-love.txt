Words from Eminem
When I heard [Just Blaze] making that track and he brought the idea to me, it was like, “What the fuck. This guy is a genius,” ’cause the fact that he took a record that's like that and made it into this, it's like, “Wow.”
I had heard that [Lil Wayne] wanted to work with me and, obviously, I wanted to work with him. When we first did the “Forever” record, it felt like, “Okay, this is cool,” but we didn't get a chance to do a record together. So, you know, basically he hit me up and wanted me to be on the “Drop The World” record, and then we kind of just agreed to do an exchange, you know what I mean? Trivia
“No Love” was released as the album’s third single, following “Not Afraid” and “Love The Way You Lie.” 
The song marks the first Eminem track to feature a rapper from outside his label camp and affiliated family.
