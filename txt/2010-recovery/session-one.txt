Trivia
This bonus track was originally exclusive to iTunes but has since been released on other music platforms. The song is not available on physical versions of the album.
Slaughterhouse were not yet signed to Shady Records when “Session One” was released, as the group were still under contract with E1 Music at the time, though negotiations for their signing were already underway.
