Trivia
“Not Afraid” was released as the album’s lead single, marking the first lead single by Eminem to carry a serious tone and message, rather than the upbeat, humorous style of his past lead singles, such as “The Real Slim Shady” and “Without Me.”
The song became the 16th in Billboard history to debut at number one on the Billboard Hot 100.
The song won a total of seven music awards, including Best Rap Solo Performance at the 53rd Annual Grammy Awards.
