Trivia
Along with most of the production credits on the album, fans were generally surprised to see Just Blaze listed, since Eminem had traditionally used in-house producers only. Just Blaze was well known for his work with JAY-Z and Roc-A-Fella Records, and for helping create a sound that contrasted with the Shady/Aftermath style of 2000s hip-hop.
