Trivia
This bonus track was originally exclusive to iTunes but has since been released on other music platforms. The song is not available on physical versions of the album.
Near the end of the song, Eminem shouts out D12, saying, “Where’s D12? I don’t feel like doing this by myself, come on!” Since then, only one D12 song featuring Eminem—“Fame”—has been released, excluding mixtape intros.
