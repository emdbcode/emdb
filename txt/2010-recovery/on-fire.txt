Words from Eminem and Denaun Porter

After Relapse, at some point I was listening to maybe something from The Eminem Show or some shit, and goin’, “Why doesn’t my [new] shit…,” like, “It doesn’t feel like anything right now.” […] It was literally like a fucking awakening, and it happened in like fuckin’ 5 minutes, 10 minutes of listening to some of my older shit and going, “I need to feel like this again,” and the first song I did [after that] was the one Mr. Porter did, “On Fire.” — Eminem, Paul Pod: Curtain Call 2, 2022

At this time, “On Fire” was him coming out of the accents, and I remember [saying], “Yo bro, let's just do it like Hip-Hop Shop — straightforward, no accents… straight spitting.”
— Denaun Porter, Paul Pod: Curtain Call 2, 2022

Trivia

On Fire” is the first Eminem song produced by Denaun Porter since the Slim Shady EP, excluding his co-production credit on “When the Music Stops” from The Eminem Show.
