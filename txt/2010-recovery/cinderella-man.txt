Trivia

The song’s title is based on American boxer James J. Braddock, who went from a down-on-his-luck dockworker during the Great Depression to the heavyweight champion, earning the nickname “Cinderella Man.”

In the song, Eminem disrespects his previous album Relapse by saying it’s in his trash.
