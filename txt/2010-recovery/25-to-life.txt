“25 To Life” is basically describing my relationship with hip-hop. To sum it up in a nutshell, at the end it says, “When it comes to people who meant the most to you, you left me off your list.” If anything, I took it as kind of a wake-up call for me to say I need to do better.” — Eminem, Shade 45, 2010
Trivia
“25 To Life” is often compared to “I Used to Love H.E.R.” by Common, as both use a narrative twist that has been common in hip-hop since Common introduced it in 1994.
