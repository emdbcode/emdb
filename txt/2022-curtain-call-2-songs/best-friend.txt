Trivia
“Best Friend” was released as the fifth single from Yelawolf’s album <em>Love Story</em> and is the only song, aside from Eminem and Bad Meets Evil tracks, to appear on Eminem’s second greatest hits album, <em>Curtain Call 2</em>.
Words from Yelawolf
The song was already really strong, and then I knew that I was touching on something that was serious, or to me it was, and the melody in the song, it came together, and I just thought “If Marshall gets on this and understands this concept, that it would be out of here.” So I just crossed my fingers and sent it to him. Luckily, he understood it perfectly and obviously killed it. It’s the perfect record for him and I, for this album, because it’s me standing my own ground creatively and where I’m at creatively, and Marshall being Marshall — so it’s like [me] to not rap… I don’t keep up with that dude. I can rock with him, but nobody can actually keep up with him. He’s an asshole, so don’t print that. He doesn’t even deserve for me to tell him how good he is.. — Yelawolf, MTV, 2015
