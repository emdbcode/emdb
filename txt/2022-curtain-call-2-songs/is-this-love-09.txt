“Is This Love (’09)” was first recorded circa 2009 during the <em>Relapse</em> sessions and completed circa 2022 for inclusion on <em>Curtain Call 2</em>.

The song marks the first Eminem song to feature 50 Cent since “Crack A Bottle” in 2009.

A demo version of the song leaked in 2025, featuring Dr. Dre and alternate lyrics delivered in Eminem’s “Relapse accent.”

Words from Eminem

The verses were right around Relapse time. […] I remember I didn't want to use it for the album because the accents weren’t thick enough. […] I felt like that’s the only thing from that time period that would’ve stood the test of time the best. — Eminem, Paul Pod: Curtain Call 2, 2022
