Words from Eminem
This song is just another one of those tracks that we did one day just fucking around. I wrote the rhyme in about twenty minutes. All three verses. […] I hummed Jeff the bass line. I wanted to touch on how last year I was always fucked up. Life was like a big party for me. It was the first year that I blew up, and I did a lot of celebrating. — Eminem, Angry Blonde, 2000

Trivia
“Drug Ballad” is the second track 13 on an Eminem album to feature Dina Rae, following “Cum On Everybody” from The Slim Shady LP.
