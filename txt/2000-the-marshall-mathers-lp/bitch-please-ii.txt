Trivia
”Bitch Please II" serves as a sequel to "Bitch Please" by Snoop Dogg, released in 1999 on No Limit Top Dogg, featuring Xzibit and Nate Dogg and produced by Dr. Dre.
The song was issued as a promotional single two weeks before the album’s release.

• According to Eminem and his manager Paul Rosenberg, a miscommunication regarding a potential collaboration following "Bitch Please II" led Snoop Dogg to believe that Eminem did not wish to work with him. The issue was resolved years later, with Eminem describing their belated second collab, "From The D 2 The LBC" (2022), as his "olive branch".
