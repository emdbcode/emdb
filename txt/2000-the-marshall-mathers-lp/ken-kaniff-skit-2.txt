Trivia
• In the skit, Violent J and Shaggy 2 Dope of Insane Clown Posse take turns performing oral sex on Ken Kaniff. The skit, along with the album’s preceding track, serves as a response to Insane Clown Posse’s song “Slim Anus.”
• This is the second album appearance of Ken Kaniff, with Eminem taking over the character from Aristotle.
