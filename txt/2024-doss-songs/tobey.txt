Trivia
“Tobey” was released as the album’s second single on July 2, 2024, following “Houdini,” and peaked at No. 24 on the Billboard Hot 100 and No. 29 on the UK Singles Chart.
The song’s title references actor Tobey Maguire, who portrayed Spider-Man in the original 2000s film trilogy. In the chorus, the rappers flip the concept, saying that instead of being bitten by a spider like Tobey, they were bitten by a goat — a play on the acronym for “Greatest of All Time.”
The song marks the fourth collaboration between Eminem and Big Sean, following “Detroit Vs. Everybody,” “No Favors,” and “Friday Night Cypher.”
The song’s video was directed by Cole Bennett, who co-produced the song, and previously directed the videos for “Godzilla” and “Gnat.”
