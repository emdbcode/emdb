Trivia
The skit continues the album’s overarching concept and serves as an intro to the next track, “Guilty Conscience 2.” Eminem posted the following warning on social media: “Public service announcement: <em>The Death of Slim Shady</em> is a conceptual album; therefore, if you listen to songs out of order, they might not make sense. Enjoy.”
Part of this skit was visualized in the “Basement Trailer” video, which was posted just hours before the album’s release.
