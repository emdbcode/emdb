Trivia
The song was first released to those who preordered the Eminem.com exclusive <em>Digital Limited Edition</em> of the album, available during a one-week window before the album’s release. The window reopened five days later for 24 hours with the inclusion of a new “Steve Berman” skit. It was widely released on Friday the 13th of September on the album’s <em>Expanded Mourner’s Edition</em>. 
Currently, the only physical release of the song is as a bonus track on the standard Japanese CD version of the album.
The song’s title pays homage to basketball players Kyrie Irving and Luka Dončić of the <em>Dallas Mavericks</em>.
The song was falsely reported as being produced by DJ Premier, due to the producer being name-dropped on the track and the song lacking credits until its wider release on the album’s <em>Expanded Mourner’s Edition</em>.
Eminem and 2 Chainz previously collaborated on “Chloraseptic (Remix),” a non-album single by Eminem released in 2018.
