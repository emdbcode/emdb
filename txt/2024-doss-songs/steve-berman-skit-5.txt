Trivia
This track marks the fifth “Steve Berman” skit, following the previous one released on <em>Relapse</em> in 2009, and the first not to feature Eminem.
The skit was first used in a promotional stunt for the album on July 14, 2024. A banner reading “Missing: Slim Shady — Please call 855-313-SLIM” was flown by plane. Those who dialed the number could listen to the skit through their phone. Its first inclusion on the album was on the updated version of the Eminem.com exclusive <em>Digital Limited Edition</em>, released three days after the phone promo and available for 24 hours only. It was widely released on Friday the 13th of September on the album’s <em>Expanded Mourner’s Edition</em>.
The skit’s inclusion on the album was promoted via an additional video skit posted on Eminem’s Instagram account, titled “Em Calls Steve.”
Currently, the only physical release of the skit is as a bonus track on the standard Japanese CD version of the album.
