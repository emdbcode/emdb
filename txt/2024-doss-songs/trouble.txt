Trivia
This short track serves as part of the album’s core concept and a prequel to the concept’s climax, “Guilty Conscience 2,” which remixes this song’s beat. Eminem posted the following warning on social media: “Public service announcement: <em>The Death of Slim Shady</em> is a conceptual album; therefore, if you listen to songs out of order, they might not make sense. Enjoy.”
