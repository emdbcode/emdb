Trivia
“Fuel” was released as a 7-inch single on May 22, 2025, exclusively via interscope.com. The single also includes the song’s remix, which was previously released on the album’s deluxe edition and as a Spotify-exclusive single.
As on numerous tracks throughout the album, Eminem takes shots at Diddy amid civil lawsuits and public backlash. He also hints that Diddy may have had a role in Tupac Shakur’s death, a claim he previously referenced on his MGK diss track, “Killshot.”
Eminem and JID later collaborated again on “Animals (Pt. I)” from JID’s EP <em>GDLU (Preluxe)</em>.
