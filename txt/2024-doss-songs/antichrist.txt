Trivia
“Antichrist” was first recorded circa 2005 during the <em>Curtain Call</em> sessions and completed circa 2024 for inclusion on <em>The Death Of Slim Shady (Coup De Grâce)</em>.

“Antichrist” was completed late into the album’s recording sessions, as Eminem references footage of Diddy assaulting R&B singer and ex-partner Cassie, which was released in May 2024.

The song marks the first Eminem track to feature a D12 member in 20 years, since <em>Encore</em> in 2004.

Bizarre is only credited as a featured artist on the album’s UK limited edition cassette.
