Trivia
The song was first released on the album’s <em>UK Limited Edition</em> cassette as a hidden track, and to those who preordered the Eminem.com exclusive <em>Digital Limited Edition</em> of the album, available during a one-week window before the album’s release. The window reopened five days later for 24 hours with the inclusion of a new “Steve Berman” skit. It was widely released on Friday the 13th of September on the album’s <em>Expanded Mourner’s Edition</em>.
