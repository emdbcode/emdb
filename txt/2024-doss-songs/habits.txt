Trivia
In “Habits,” Eminem tackles censorship and explores his struggles with addiction, beginning with the song’s opening skit where he is forced to take a pill, which ties into the album’s overarching concept. Eminem posted the following warning on social media: “Public service announcement: <em>The Death of Slim Shady</em> is a conceptual album; therefore, if you listen to songs out of order, they might not make sense. Enjoy.”
