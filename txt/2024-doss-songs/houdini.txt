Trivia
Houdini was released as the albums lead single on May 31, 2024, peaking at No. 2 on the billboard hot 100 and topping the charts in multiple countries worldwide including the UK, Switzerland, Portugal, Norway, New Zealand, Hungary, Canada, and Australia.
The song marks the first “funny” lead single released by Eminem since “We Made You” in 2009, a trope he used to promote the release of his first five major-label studio albums.
The title “Houdini” references legendary escape artist Harry Houdini. Eminem previously referenced Harry Houdini in the “Role Model” video, featuring a silent film–style underwater escape scene.
In the song, Eminem uses the “guess who’s back” line for the third time in the intro of a lead single, following “Without Me” and “Just Lose It.”
The song’s video serves as a sequel to the “Without Me” video, with Eminem reprising his role as Rapboy. This time, he and Dr. Dre must stop Slim Shady from trying to get them cancelled.
The music video is packed with cameos from the likes of 50 Cent, Alchemist, Dr. Dre, Ez Mil, Jimmy Iovine, Pete Davidson, Paul Rosenberg, Royce Da 5’9”, Shane Gillis, Snoop Dogg, and Westside Boogie.
The track marks the first Eminem song to have a “Paul” skit attached to it.
The artwork for the song’s single was created by Mike Saputo, who also designed the artwork for Eminem’s previous albums <em>Kamikaze</em>, <em>Music to Be Murdered By</em>, and <em>Curtain Call 2</em>.
