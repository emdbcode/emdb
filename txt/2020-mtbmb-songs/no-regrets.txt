Trivia
In “No Regrets,” Eminem touches on the diss he made against Tyler, the Creator, and Earl Sweatshirt, which drew backlash due to the use of a homophobic slur against Tyler, who has publicly discussed his sexuality and same-sex experiences.
Words from Don Toliver
I remember that day—that was crazy. I was in the studio randomly, and we just got a call to do that record. It was so last minute. I forgot how, but it was like a clutch or something, where I had to record at that moment and send it off. And I ended up being on the album, man. I’m forever, forever thankful, for sure. — Don Toliver, Hot 97 FM, 2021
