Trivia
The music video for “Higher” was released on January 23, 2021, premiering during UFC 257: Poirier vs. McGregor 2. It features UFC footage and includes an official ESPN skit with a cameo from Dana White, CEO and president of the UFC. The video also pays homage to LL Cool J’s 1991 video for “Mama Said Knock You Out”.
