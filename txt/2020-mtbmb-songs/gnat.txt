Trivia
The “Gnat” video was released less than an hour after the album was surprise released, effectively acting in place of a lead single due to its same-day release as the album. The song peaked at No. 60 on the Billboard Hot 100 and No. 65 on the UK Singles Chart.
Gnat is Eminem’s COVID-19 track, reflecting the global situation at the time of its recording and release.
In the song’s chorus, the line “They come at me with machine guns, it’s like trying to fight off a gnat” is widely interpreted as a shot at Machine Gun Kelly, who responded by tweeting, “I’m under your skin,” alongside an upside-down smiley face emoji.
The video was directed by Cole Bennett, who previously directed the “Godzilla” music video and later directed the “Tobey” video from his next album, <em>The Death of Slim Shady</em>.
