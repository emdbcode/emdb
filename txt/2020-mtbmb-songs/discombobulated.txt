Trivia
“Discombobulated” was first recorded circa 2009 during the Relapse sessions and completed circa 2020 for Music To Be Murdered By - Side B (Deluxe Edition).
In January 2025, the unmastered, unfinished <em>Relapse</em> version leaked online.
Frano’s contribution to the song had previously been released in 2018 via SoundCloud under the title “Outlinedfaces 157 BPM.” It was part of his sample pack, <em>Frano Vol. 1</em>, released through Frank Dukes’ sample library, Kingsway Music.
