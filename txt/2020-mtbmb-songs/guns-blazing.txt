Trivia
“Guns Blazing” was recorded alongside the Dr. Dre track “Gospel.” The two songs were later traded, with “Guns Blazing” intended for Dr. Dre’s <em>Grand Theft Auto</em> project <em>The Contract</em>, and “Gospel” for Eminem’s <em>Music to Be Murdered By – Side B</em>. Eminem acquired the track, stating that it “fit the theme of the album a little better to me” than “Gospel.”
