Trivia
The song marks the third in a three-track run produced by Dr. Dre, following “Never Love Again” and “Lock It Up.” The last time Eminem released three Dr. Dre-produced tracks consecutively was on <em>Relapse: Refill</em> in 2009.
Eminem and Anderson .Paak previously collaborated on Dr. Dre’s “Medicine Man” from <em>Compton</em>, as well as the officially unreleased track “Freak.”
