Trivia
In “Marsh,” Eminem plays on his real name, Marshall, and “Martian,” an alien from Mars, rapping “My name is Marsh and, I’m out this world,” which doubles as “My name is Martian, I’m out this world.”
In the song, Eminem takes shots at Machine Gun Kelly in response to his public statements about Eminem’s diss track “Killshot.” He also comically imitates Travis Scott, using auto-tuned ad-libs in the chorus, including Scott’s signature “It’s lit!”
