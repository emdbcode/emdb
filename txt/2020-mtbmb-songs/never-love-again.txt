Trivia
In “Never Love Again,” Eminem uses the guise of a traditional breakup song to describe his relationship with drug addiction.
The song marks the first in a three-track run produced by Dr. Dre, followed by “Little Engine” and “Lock It Up.” The last time Eminem released three Dr. Dre-produced tracks consecutively was on <em>Relapse: Refill</em> in 2009.
