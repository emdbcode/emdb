Trivia
"Zeus" gained a lot of attention from fans and made headlines due to Eminem dissing Snoop Dogg on the song, following Snoop Dogg's shocking reveal of how he values Eminem's music. The beef was surprising because both rappers share a close relationship with Dr. Dre. Snoop Dogg responded to the song via Instagram, commenting on an Eminem fan post: “Pray I don’t answer that soft ass shit.” The two immediately squashed the beef following Dr. Dre’s near-fatal aneurysm days after Snoop Dogg’s post, with Eminem and Paul Rosenberg later revealing that Snoop Dogg had been shunning Eminem due to a miscommunication following their collaboration on "Bitch Please II" in 2000.
Words from Eminem
Everything [Snoop] said, by the way, was fine, up to a point. […] It was the last statement where he said, “Far as music I can live without, I can live without that shit.” Now you’re being disrespectful. It just caught me off-guard. I wasn’t ready for that. — Eminem, Shade 45, 2020
