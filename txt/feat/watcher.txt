Trivia
The song was released as a single exclusively in France on February 27, 2001, where it peaked at No. 47 on the singles chart.
One of Dr. Dre’s lines from the song was later interpolated into the chorus of the Ice Cube track “Hello,” which featured fellow N.W.A members Dr. Dre and MC Ren.
A sequel, “The Watcher 2,” was released in 2002 by JAY-Z on his seventh studio album <em>The Blueprint 2: The Gift & The Curse</em>, featuring Dr. Dre and Rakim.
A remix of “The Watcher 2,” titled “The Watcher 3,” featuring Snoop Dogg in place of JAY-Z and including a new bridge, was released in 2003 as a white-label single to promote the DJ Jam mixtape <em>WBALLZ 187.4 FM Vol. 1</em>. The mixtape features an edited version of the song, with Rakim’s verse omitted.
Although Eminem wrote lines that were later interpolated into Ice Cube’s “Hello” and JAY-Z’s “The Watcher 2,” he did not receive a writing credit on either song.
