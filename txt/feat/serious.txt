Trivia
Promatic was a rap duo consisting of D12’s Proof and Sicknotes affiliate Dogmatic.
The song features an uncredited rap verse from D12’s Swifty McVay.
This version is often dubbed a remix, as a version without Eminem and Swifty was released as a hidden track on Promatic’s self-titled studio album.
