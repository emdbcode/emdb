Trivia
“Don’t Approach Me” marks the fourth collaboration between Eminem and Xzibit, following “The Anthem,” “What’s The Difference,” and “Bitch Please II.” The two later collaborated on a fifth track, “My Name,” also featuring Nate Dogg, who appeared with them on “Bitch Please II.”
