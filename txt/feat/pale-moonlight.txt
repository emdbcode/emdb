Trivia
This song was only released in demo/unfinished form.
A two-minute sampler first appeared on DJ King David’s <em>Detroit Streets Vol. 6</em> mixtape in 2005, with the same version also used on Strike’s <em>Street Sampler</em> CD released around the same time.
Strike later re-released the song in 2007 on his mixtape <em>Strike 1 — The Mixtape</em>.
