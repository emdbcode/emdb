Trivia
A remix of the song was released on the “We Shine” white-label single. A second remix appeared on Da Ruckus’ compilation album <em>Da Forgotten Episode: Bootlegs, Breaks & B-Sides</em>.
Hush of Da Ruckus later collaborated with Eminem again in 2005 on the song “Off To Tijuana,” which also featured D12’s Kuniva and Swifty McVay.
