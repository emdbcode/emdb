Trivia
This is an alternate version of a Royce Da 5’9” track found on his album <em>Rock City</em>. The album version, released prior to this version, features Tre’ Little in place of Eminem. However, Eminem can still be heard saying “asshole” at the very start of the track.
The song leaked before Royce decided to use it and appeared on unofficial mixtapes prior to its inclusion on Royce’s <em>Build & Destroy</em> mixtape.
It was later omitted from the <em>Build & Destroy</em> project when the mixtape was reissued as a compilation album by Trouble Records on July 29, 2003.
