Trivia
“Realest” marks Ez Mil’s major-label debut single and his first collaboration with Eminem. He was signed by Eminem and Dr. Dre to Shady / Aftermath in July 2023, becoming the first artist to do so since Stat Quo in 2003.
Words from Ez Mil
I had already made the beat for “Realest” [before signing to Shady / Aftermath]. It had the first verse, I think, and a hook. […] I played it for [Em and Dre]. Dre was bobbing and right after the beat was playing he was like, “Yep, Marshall’s gonna eat that up”. And then Em was at the back [and said], “Yep, give it to me.” — Ez Mil, RX931, 2023
