Trivia
The song was re-released on Da Ruckus’ 2022 compilation album <em>Da Unreleased Episode</em> under the title “We Shine (Ruff & Rugged Remix).”
An alternate remix appeared on Da Ruckus’ compilation album <em>Da Forgotten Episode: Bootlegs, Breaks & B-Sides</em>.
Hush of Da Ruckus later collaborated with Eminem again in 2005 on the song “Off To Tijuana,” which also featured D12’s Kuniva and Swifty McVay.
