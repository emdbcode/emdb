Trivia
“No Favors” marks the second collaboration between Big Sean and Eminem, following “Detroit Vs. Everybody” from <em>SHADYXV</em>.
Eminem takes aim at Donald Trump on the track, as he did in his previous song “Campaign Speech” and would again multiple times in 2017, including on his album <em>Revival</em> and in his BET Cypher.
Words from Big Sean
[Eminem] was the only person I heard that could be on that song. It kinda reminded me of why I am such an Eminem fan. It reminded me of why I fell in love with Eminem’s work. He is unique, he’s special. — Big Sean, Beats1, 2017
Being on a song with [Eminem] was crazy… You know, Eminem… I didn’t know if he was going to be able to get to it in time. A couple weeks went by, [then] he sent the verse back. Crazy. He just popped out of nowhere… I’m such a fan that it just meant a lot for me. Because I know Em don’t do features like that. It’s a short list of people that get that Eminem feature, and on this song, he had so much to say. He let it all out. — Big Sean, Genius, 2017
