Trivia
In the song, Juice WRLD reflects heavily on pill addiction, while Eminem raps about the repercussions based on his own experience and the deaths of other performers, including Juice WRLD.
The song marks the second collaboration between Juice WRLD and Eminem, following “Godzilla,” although Eminem’s verse was recorded after Juice WRLD’s passing. Eminem was also sampled in a similar-themed interlude titled “Eminem Speaks” on Juice WRLD’s previous album, though it was not a collaborative performance.
