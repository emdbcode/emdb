Trivia

This skit plays a portion of Ja Rule’s freestyle verse from “Murda Freestyle,” in which he disses Eminem.

W Below the Radar, the fictional radio station mentioned in the skit, is a reference to W Balls, a fictional radio station featured in numerous Snoop Dogg songs. W Balls is described as “the station that plays only platinum hits,” while W Below the Radar is said to be “bringing you all the latest jealous misses in Hip-Hop and R&B.”
