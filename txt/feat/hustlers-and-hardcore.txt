Trivia 
The unedited original version of the song is currently unavailable on official streaming platforms and can only be obtained officially via the original CD release. Only an edited version, with Feel-X’s vocals removed, is available to stream. Unofficial uploads of the original track to YouTube are blocked by Domingo Padilla.
The song was remixed the following year as “Hellbound (H&H Remix).” Eminem’s vocals were the only element carried over from the original track.
