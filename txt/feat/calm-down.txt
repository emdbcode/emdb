Trivia
The song marks the first officially released collaboration between Busta Rhymes and Eminem. An earlier collaboration, titled “I’ll Hurt You,” was recorded circa 2005 but went officially unreleased.
The song was later included on the deluxe version of Busta Rhymes’ tenth studio album <em>Extinction Level Event 2: The Wrath Of God (Reloaded)</em> in November 2020.
