Trivia
“Roman’s Revenge” was made available as an iTunes promotional release ahead of the album’s full tracklist.
A remix featuring Lil Wayne in place of Eminem was released as an iTunes-exclusive single in January 2011 and later included on the album’s digital deluxe edition that same year.
