Trivia

This track is a remix of Cordae’s 2020 non-album single “The Parables.”

In a 2022 interview with <em>Apple Music</em>, Cordae revealed that his initial plan was to feature Eminem alongside Lil Wayne on the song “Sinister,” stating, “like Drake did it on ‘Forever,’” but he ultimately decided to place each rapper on tracks better suited to them, with Wayne remaining on “Sinister” and Em appearing on “Parables (Remix).”
