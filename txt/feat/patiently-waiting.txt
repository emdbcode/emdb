Trivia
Despite not being released as a single, the song still managed to chart at No. 56 on the Billboard Hot R&B/Hip-Hop chart due to strong airplay on urban radio stations.
Words from Eminem
[When 50 recorded it], “Patiently Waiting” just had the strings on it and the drums. That’s one of the fun things for me to do as a producer, is to hear somebody's rhyme and what they've done, and then kind of build around that, as opposed to building the beat first. I was really into trying to make it cinematic. I hadn’t planned on being on the song yet. I just wanted to hear him do something to it, and then when I heard what he did to it, I was like, “Oh, maybe I could be on this one.”
I was trying to let anyone who didn't know the story of what had gone on with 50 kind of understand it — like trying to explain a little bit of that, like, “Look, this is what he's been through and this is where he's at now,” and “this is the next dude, this is the next guy.” — Eminem, Complex, 2014
