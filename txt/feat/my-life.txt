Trivia
“My Life” was released as the third single from 50 Cent’s scrapped studio album <em>Street King Immortal</em>.
Before the song’s release, 50 Cent revealed that the beat was initially created for Dr. Dre’s scrapped <em>Detox</em> project.
In the song, 50 Cent takes shots at former G-Unit members The Game and Young Buck, stating that when they are next heard, they may sound different because he is no longer writing hits for them.
The song marks the final collaboration between Eminem and 50 Cent during 50 Cent’s time on Interscope and under Eminem’s Shady Records.
The song peaked. At No. 27 on the Billboard Hot 100 and No. 2 on the UK Singles Chart.
