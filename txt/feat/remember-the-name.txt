Trivia
“Remember The Name” marks the third collaboration between Ed Sheeran and Eminem, and the first collaboration between Eminem and 50 Cent since “My Life” in 2012.
The song was released as a streaming single exclusively on Spotify.
The song’s existence was announced by 50 Cent in December 2018 via Twitter.
