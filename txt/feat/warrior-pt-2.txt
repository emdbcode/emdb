Trivia
The song serves as a sequel — or “remix,” as stated by Eminem in the track — to Lloyd Banks’ “Warrior,” both from his debut album <em>The Hunger for More</em>.
The song marks the fourth collaboration between Lloyd Banks and Eminem, following “Don’t Push Me,” “Bump Heads,” and “We All Die One Day,” all of which also feature 50 Cent. The two would later collaborate again on “You Don’t Know” and “Where I’m At.”
