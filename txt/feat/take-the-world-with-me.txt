Trivia

The song was at one point considered for placement on <em>The Slim Shady LP</em>, as a lightly remixed version with additional production appeared on a leaked demo version of the album titled <em>Ruff Mixes #2</em>. The leak was later unofficially packaged as a fake promotional release titled <em>Unmastered Sequence</em>.
