Trivia
“Watch Deez” was later made available on Thirstin Howl III’s albums <em>Skillosopher</em> and <em>Skilligan’s Island</em> in 2000 and 2002, respectively.
The song is one of two tracks featuring Eminem released by Rawkus Records in 1999, the other being “Any Man,” which appeared on <em>Rawkus Presents: Soundbombing II</em>.
