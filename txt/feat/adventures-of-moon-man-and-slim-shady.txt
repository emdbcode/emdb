Trivia
The song was released as a non-album single, peaking at No. 22 on the Billboard Hot 100 and No. 44 on the UK Singles Chart.
The song marks the first official collaboration between Eminem and Kid Cudi. However, an officially unreleased track by Kid Cudi and Lil B, with production by Eminem, leaked sometime in 2009.
Eminem previously sampled Kid Cudi’s track “In My Dreams 2015” on the Bad Meets Evil song “All I Think About,” and also referenced his name within a verse.
