Trivia
Eminem freestyles over an unperfected version of the beat for “Love Me” from the <em>8 Mile</em> soundtrack.
This freestyle first appeared unmastered on DJ Kay Slay’s 2002 mixtape <em>Say What You Say</em>, with an alternate intro by Eminem. The mixtape was named after the <em>The Eminem Show</em> track of the same name, on which Eminem and Dr. Dre diss Jermaine Dupri, who Eminem targets again in this freestyle.
