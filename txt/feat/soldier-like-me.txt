Trivia
The song serves as a sequel to the <em>2Pacalypse Now</em> track “Soulja’s Story,” with both songs featuring the “soldier like me” lyric in the intro, outro and chorus, a deepened 2Pac vocal, and themes of crooked cops.


2Pac’s vocals are sourced from two officially unreleased demo tracks: the second verse is taken from a song titled “Judgment Day,” while the rest of the vocals come from “Revenge Of The Soulja.”
