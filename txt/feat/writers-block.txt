Trivia
“Writer’s Block” was released as the album’s lead single, peaking at No. 4 on the Bubbling Under R&B/Hip-Hop chart.
The song marks the first Royce Da 5’9” and Eminem collaboration since “Rock City” in 2002.
A remix by DJ Premier was created and included on the digital version of the album.
The song’s music video uses both versions of the track — the original and the remix. Eminem does not appear in the video.
