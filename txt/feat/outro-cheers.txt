Lyrics
The track centers on rap beef, mostly directed at Ja Rule and Murder Inc.
In the song, Bizarre restates that he's staying out of the Ja Rule beef, as previously stated in “Keep Talkin’,” released earlier that same year. He was also the only D12 member absent from the Ja Rule diss track “Doe Rae Me,” released the same day as “Keep Talkin’.”
At the end of the song, Bizarre teases D12’s next album as <em>Devil’s Night 2</em>; it was ultimately released under the title <em>D12 World</em>.
