Trivia

<em>The Devil’s Night Mixtape</em> was originally issued without this Eminem introduction; the track was added the following day on an updated version subtitled <em>Reloaded</em>. The “Reloaded” subtitle was later removed for the 2024 streaming release, where the standard version now includes the introduction under the title “The Intro And Em.”
