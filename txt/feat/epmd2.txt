Trivia
“EPMD 2” is a sequel to Nas’s 2021 single, with both songs named after the hip-hop duo who appear on this sequel.
The song marks the first time Eminem and Nas officially appear together on the same track. The two had previously collaborated on Nas’s “The Cross,” which features vocals by Nas and production by Eminem. They also appear together on the leaked Dr. Dre track “Topless,” which features Nas and reference verses by Eminem and T.I., performing as Dr. Dre.
