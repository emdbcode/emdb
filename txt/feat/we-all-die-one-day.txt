Trivia
This song was initially recorded for DJ Muggs and was set to appear on the third <em>Soul Assassins</em> album, which was ultimately scrapped. Although a third <em>Soul Assassins</em> album was later released, it had evolved into a different project by that time.
The track was first released on DJ Green Lantern’s mixtape <em>Invasion Part II: Conspiracy Theory</em>, credited to DJ Muggs and featuring additional vocals from Green Lantern, shouting out DJ Muggs and <em>Soul Assassins</em>.
