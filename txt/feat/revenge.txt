Trivia
A music video for the song was created but ultimately scrapped. The video has since leaked and features a number of fake Slim Shadys in place of the real Slim Shady. P!nk said of the video: “We shot it, and it was terrible. It’s never coming out. It just didn’t work, and it was the wrong timing for it. I looked like hell. It was awful. Thank God Eminem wasn’t in it.”
Words from P!nk
I reached out to [Eminem] in this case. I love him. I’ve always loved him. I think he’s a lyrical genius, and I just wrote him a love letter. I went to the studio, I drank a lot of wine, I wrote “Revenge,” I went home, and then I drank more wine. Then I wrote him an email: “I love you. I’ve always loved you. I’ve loved you since you gave me your autograph at the 2001 MTV Video Music Awards, and I love that you always work with the same people.” And he just wrote back, “Okay.” It’s a funny song. It’s fun. There is nothing serious about it. My mom loves it. — P!nk, <em>Lorraine</em>, 2017
