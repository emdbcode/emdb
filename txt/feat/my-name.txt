Trivia
“My Name” marks the fifth collaboration between Eminem and Xzibit, following “The Anthem,” “What’s The Difference,” “Bitch Please II,” and “Don’t Approach Me. The song also marks the third collaboration between Eminem and Nate Dogg, following “Bitch Please II” and “’Till I Collapse.”
Xzibit and Eminem take aim at Jermaine Dupri in the song, in retaliation for a freestyle Dupri released dissing Eminem and Dr. Dre, which itself responded to the Eminem song “Say What You Say.” Eminem also takes shots at Canibus.
Eminem states in the song that he only tapped Dupri in “Say What You Say” and didn’t fully go at him, leaving that to Dre — which aligns with the track.
Words from Xzibit
Me and Eminem shit in on Jermaine Dupri and it was just because we were sitting in the studio one day and [Dupri] comes on talking shit about Dr. Dre. Dre turned around and said, “Y’all got this?” How the fuck am I supposed to react with that, you know what I’m saying? You gotta go get that nigga. — Xzibit, Effective Immediately, 2025
