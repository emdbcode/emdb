Trivia
This version of the song features alternate spoken lines by Eminem compared to the original version.
The song was featured on both the “Highlife” and “Can’t Get the Best of Me” singles, which were released simultaneously, although it was not included on their joint double A-side single, instead appearing on the joint “Highlife (Fredwreck Remix)” / “Can’t Get the Best of Me” single.
The track was later included on Cypress Hill’s “Lowrider” single in 2002 and again on their <em>Stash EP</em> later that same year.
