Trivia
“I Need A Doctor” was initially released as the second single from Dr. Dre’s proposed third studio album <em>Detox</em>, following “Kush.” As the album was ultimately scrapped, the track is considered a non-album single.
The song peaked at No. 4 on the Billboard Hot 100, marking his highest placement on the chart since “Nuthin’ But A ‘G’ Thang” in 1993.
The song has been noted for its similarity to “Love the Way You Lie,” featuring a Skylar Grey–written chorus and an Alex da Kid–produced beat.
Words from Skylar Grey
I was working with Alex a lot at that point, and [Em] was working on Dre’s album. Alex got invited out, and he brought me with him, and we presented “I Need a Doctor” — that was when I met Em for the first time. I was really nervous, very quiet. You know, I grew up listening to Em — I mean, “Stan” was one of my favorite songs of all time — so to be working with him was a complete dream come true. [Dre] was so sweet — I couldn’t believe how kind he was. I remember him sitting me down in the kitchen, just by myself, and he was just like, “Thank you so much for coming and working on this.” I was like, “What’s going on? Am I in a dream?”
— Skylar Grey, Paul Pod: Curtain Call 2, 2022
