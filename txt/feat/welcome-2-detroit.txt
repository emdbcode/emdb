Trivia
“Welcome 2 Detroit” first appeared unmastered on Clinton Sparks’ <em>Anger Management 3</em> mixtape under the title “Welcome To Detroit City.”
The song was released as a single on October 11, 2005, peaking at No. 100 on the Billboard Hot 100.
Late D12 member Proof, who was also a member of the rap group Goon Squad with Trick Trick, appears in the song’s video.
