Trivia
In “Hip Hop,” Bizarre recounts his upbringing with hip-hop before shifting to rap beefs and guns. Eminem continues the gun theme, basing his entire verse around firearms and referencing his pistol-whipping incident with John Guerra.
The song marks the second Bizarre song to feature Eminem, following “Trife Thieves” released in 1998.
