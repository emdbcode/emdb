Trivia
"Dumpin" was recorded in 1997 after Proof and Eminem asked B-Flat and Dirrty Ratt of Parts Unknown to join D12, which at the time also featured Eye-Kyu, who appears on the track as well.

The song was recorded on a TASCAM 4-track recorder.
