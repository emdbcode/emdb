Trivia
“Forgot About Dre” was released as the second single from Dr. Dre’s second studio album <em>2001</em> on January 29, 2000. The song peaked at No. 25 on the Billboard Hot 100 and No. 7 on the UK Singles Chart.
The single version features an additional outro with a rap verse from Hittman, subtitled the “Director’s Cut Video Version,” as it was used for the director’s cut of the video.
Words from Dr. Dre
Every now and then, an artist comes in with the lyrics first, and that has only happened a couple of times in my career — one of those times was with Eminem on “Forgot About Dre.” He had the entire song written before the beat, so we had to make the beat work with his lyrics. — Dr. Dre, Jimmy Kimmel Live, 2024
