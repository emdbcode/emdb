Trivia
Eminem has often cited Redman as one of his biggest influences, even placing him on his list of greatest rappers, as heard in the song “’Till I Collapse.” Redman has shown the same level of respect for Eminem, stating that he ranks him alongside Biggie, Jay-Z, and Nas as one of the greatest rappers of all time.
The song was used as the title track of a popular Eminem bootleg album in the 2000s, and was listed alongside other Eminem features and rarities.
Eminem previously sampled Redman’s voice from “Soopaman Luva 3” for his song “Low, Down Dirty.” He also borrowed a line from Redman’s “Rated ‘R’” for the track, which ironically refers to stealing raps.
Words from Eminem
I don’t think that enough people heard “Off The Wall”, the song I did with Redman. It was a good going-back-and-forth type thing. “Causin' terror to Christina Aguilera / Grab her by the hair and drag her across the Sahara”. I know I used that. That’s ill. That’s some caveman shit. — Eminem, The Way I Am, 2008
