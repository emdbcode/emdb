Trivia
(Rap) Superstar was released as the lead single from Cypress Hill’s fifth studio album Skull & Bones.
Two remixes of the song were created: the Alchemist Remix, released as the B-side of the album’s second single “Highlife,” and “(Rock) Superstar,” which does not feature Eminem or Noreaga and was released on disc two of the <em>Skull & Bones</em> album.
Eminem and Noreaga were not credited on the song’s physical releases, although some singles included a hype sticker stating, “includes testimonials from Eminem and Noreaga.”
