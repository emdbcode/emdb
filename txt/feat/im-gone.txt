Trivia
“I’m Gone” is the last diss track aimed at Benzino that Eminem released before suggesting that both sides step away from the beef in “Like Toy Soldiers” and “Gatman And Robbin’.” Eminem has since taken shots at Benzino, but not in the form of a full-fledged, name-saying diss track.
