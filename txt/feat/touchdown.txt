Trivia
“Touchdown” marks the first collaboration between T.I. and Eminem. The two later collaborated again on “That’s All She Wrote” from T.I.’s seventh studio album <em>No Mercy</em>. They also each contributed reference verses for Dr. Dre’s officially unreleased <em>Detox</em> track “Topless,” which features Nas.
