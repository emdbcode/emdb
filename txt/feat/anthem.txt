Trivia

Eminem was also featured on a second track from the <em>This Or That</em> album, “Get You Mad,” which was later included on <em>The Slim Shady LP</em> (Special Edition) that same year.
Eminem later collaborated with Tech N9ne on “Speedom (WWC2)” and with Xzibit on “What’s the Difference?,” “Bitch Please II,” “Don’t Approach Me,” and “My Name.”

Words from Sway

Me and Tech, we had these visions, man, way ahead of the game. We could play everybody. Anybody who was popular at that time would come by the show. So when we did “The Anthem,” it was like calling together the Avengers. We called on everybody that we thought was dope and that represented the [Wake Up] Show, and we had everybody together for that one video. — Sway, Sway’s Universe, 2025
