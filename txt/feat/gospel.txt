Trivia
“Gospel” was promoted as part of the GTA Online: The Contract soundtrack, however the collection was only issued as a playlist compiling singles from the project.

The song was recorded alongside the Eminem track “Guns Blazing.” The two songs were later traded, with “Guns Blazing” originally intended for Dr. Dre’s <em>Grand Theft Auto</em> project and “Gospel” for the deluxe version of Eminem’s <em>Music To Be Murdered By</em>. Eminem requested “Guns Blazing,” stating, “it fit the theme of [my] album a little better.”
