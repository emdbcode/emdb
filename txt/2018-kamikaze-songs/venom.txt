Trivia
“Venom” was released as a single on September 21, 2018, peaking at No. 43 on the Billboard Hot 100 and No. 16 on the UK Singles Chart.
The song was written for the Marvel movie Venom — a spin-off set in the Spider-Man universe, starring Tom Hardy.
The song’s video acts as a spin-off from Eminem’s previous video, “Fall.” In “Fall,” Eminem is chased by an all-black symbiote, ultimately getting caught and becoming its host. He then steps on a discarded Revival CD, crushing it. In the “Venom” video, a man picks up the CD, becomes infected by the symbiote, and is uncontrollably forced to rap like Eminem. The symbiote then jumps from person to person throughout the video.
A remix of the song, produced by d.a. got that dope, was released in 2021on Skyler Grey’s “Last One Standing” single, which was created for the <em>Venom</em> film sequel, <em>Venom: Let There Be Carnage</em>.
