Trivia
Eminem’s headline set at Tramps in New York was recorded on January 9, 1999, and also featured performances by Sir Menelik, MF DOOM, and A-Mob.
The performance was filmed and later released on a DVD included with The Eminem Show (Limited Edition) in 2002.
