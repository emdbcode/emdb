Trivia
In 2015, Kuniva stated that the song’s instrumental was originally intended for D12’s debut studio album, Devil’s Night, supporting Eminem’s recollection that he had made the beat for Bizarre.
According to their accounts, Bizarre planned to take the track in a more extreme direction. After receiving pushback from the rest of D12, he abandoned the idea, leaving the instrumental available for Eminem to use.
In 2013,  Eminem apologised to his mother for “Cleanin’ Out My Closet” in the song “Headlights” from The Marshall Mathers LP 2.
