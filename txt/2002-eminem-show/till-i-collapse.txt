Trivia
“Till I Collapse” marks the second collaboration between Eminem and Nate Dogg, following “Bitch Please II” from The Marshall Mathers LP.
Because the song was only released as a limited promotional club single, its popularity was difficult to measure at the time. With the rise of streaming, the song eventually charted in multiple countries years after its original release.
As of 2026, the track has surpassed 2.4 billion streams, making it the third most popular song by Eminem.
The song samples the drum beat from “We Will Rock You” by Queen. Eminem later revisited the sample on his next album, Encore, on the track “Puke.”
“Till I Collapse” is featured in the 2011 film Real Steel and on its soundtrack album, along with the 2011 Bad Meets Evil single “Fast Lane.”
