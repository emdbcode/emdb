Trivia
“Soldier” is the second track in a three-part sequence, beginning with the skit “The Kiss” and concluding with “Say Goodbye Hollywood.” Together, the tracks chronicle Eminem’s altercation with bouncer John Guerra after witnessing him kiss Kim. Eminem later revisits the incident on “Sing for the Moment.”
