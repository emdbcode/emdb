Trivia
“Superman” marks the fourth and final track 13 to feature Eminem alongside singer Dina Rae, following “Cum On Everybody,” “Drug Ballad,” and D12’s “Pimp Like Me.”
Vocals recorded by Mariah Carey during sessions for the song were later used in Eminem’s 2009 diss track, “The Warning.”
“Superman” became the second Eminem promotional single to receive a music video, following “Role Model.”
Although it is the third Eminem solo track to feature vocals by Dina Rae, it is the first to credit her as a featured artist. However, her name appears in smaller font than the album’s other featured artists, and her credit is omitted from the promotional singles.
