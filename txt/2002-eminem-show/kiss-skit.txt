Triva
“The Kiss” is the opening skit in a three-track sequence, followed by “Soldier” and “Say Goodbye Hollywood.” Together, these tracks chronicle Eminem’s altercation with bouncer John Guerra after witnessing him kiss Kim.
The song playing in the car is “Everybody’s Looking At Me” by Eminem. The track remained unreleased for 23 years before finally appearing on STANS (The Official Soundtrack) in 2025.
