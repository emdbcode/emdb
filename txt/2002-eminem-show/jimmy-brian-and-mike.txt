Trivia
“Jimmy, Brian And Mike” was first recorded circa 1999 during sessions for The Marshall Mathers LP and later completed around 2022 for The Eminem Show (Expanded Edition).
The song appeared on leaked track listings for The Marshall Mathers LP in 2000. It is theorized that these were merely working lists of songs recorded during the sessions, rather than tracklists with an official running order.
