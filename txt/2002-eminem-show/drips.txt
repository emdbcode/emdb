Trivia
Due to the song’s explicit subject matter, “Drips” was omitted from the album’s clean version.
The song was later sampled in Obie Trice’s debut single, “Rap Name,” which itself is sampled in Eminem’s “Without Me.”
It is theorized that “Drips” was created first, followed by “Rap Name” (sampling “Drips”), and then “Without Me” (sampling “Rap Name”), despite the differing release order.
