Trivia
“Business” was released as a promotional single in the US, charting on the Billboard Hip-Hop charts on July 13, 2002. It was released a year later in Europe and Australia as a physical single on July 14, 2003.
No music video was made for the song despite its release as a single. In Europe and Australia, where the song was fully promoted, live performances from London and Barcelona were broadcast on cable and satellite music channels during its chart run.
