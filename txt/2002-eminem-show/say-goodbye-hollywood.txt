Trivia
“Say Goodbye Hollywood” concludes the story begun on “The Kiss” and “Soldier,” the two preceding tracks on the album. As the last song in a three-track sequence, it serves as the aftermath of the events depicted in the earlier songs.
The song “Stimulate,” later included on The Eminem Show (Expanded Edition), was originally intended to be track 8 on The Eminem Show but was ultimately omitted and replaced by “Say Goodbye Hollywood.”
In the album insert, written by Eminem himself, the track is listed as “Sayin’ Goodbye To Hollywood.” In 2009, it was featured on DJ Hero (Renegade Edition) under the title “Say Goodbye To Hollywood.”
The song can also be heard in the 2006 film Alpha Dog, a true-story drama based on Jesse James Hollywood and the kidnapping of Nicholas Markowitz.
